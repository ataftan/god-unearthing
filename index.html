cyclical states of being

about

how we relate to one other– bodies and earth, roots and veins, skin and soil– is rooted in the mystical and incomprehensible. the technologies of the living carry speechless knowledge in anatomy, gesture, embodiment. these drawings map out entities and movements as seen in ecological cartographies of the everyday, to visualise the sensory nature of attention.
